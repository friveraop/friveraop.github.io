Overview

This is a dashboard displaying some leisure activities in NYC. The base layer for the first map is for population and the other is for houshold income. The function of this dashboard is to provide the user with an overview of local features, demography, and basic income stats in order to inform decisions for development/ buisness ventures/ other entrepreneurial activities. Use the select tool in the top left to change the indicators.

Economic and population statistics are from 2021. The Pie Chart on the Income tab shows the number of zips belonging to each borough and when hovered over, displays avg per capita income in each.

The colored indicator on the Income map tab tells the user whether the selected ZIP is a suitable place for development based on the metrics of household income and population, with a high income (> $65k) and high population (>60k) being marked as 'good' and a high income and low population still being suitable. A low income and high pop is 'decent' and a zip with both low population and low income is unfavorable.
